about me

When I was young, I loved Dexter's Laboratory, a cartoon about a young boy who invents the unimaginable in his attic. I dreamed of achieving the same things in my own life and devoted myself to the study of science. The further I got into the sciences, the more I realized that the magic in Dexter's life wasn't his understanding of the world, it was his ability to create something from nothing--to breathe life into his work. Since I became a programmer, I have discovered the same skill in myself--I can build applications, tools, and games from keystrokes, and I am only limited by how fast I type.
